# Player Behaviour Profiles

This document outlines the primary player archetypes based on session frequency, betting patterns, and financial behavior.

---

## 1. The Recreational Player
*The casual user playing for entertainment rather than profit.*

* **Session Frequency:** Highly variable; ranges from quick "5-20 spin" sessions to 3-4 sessions per week (several hundred spins).
* **Betting Style:** Consistently low stakes; rarely adjusts bet sizes.
* **Bet Range:** €0.10 – €1.00 (rarely higher).
* **Financials:** Deposits usually do not exceed €100 per session.
* **Exit Strategy:** Plays for a predetermined time or enjoyment level, then logs off regardless of the Win/Loss outcome.

---

## 2. The VIP
*The high-value, high-frequency player with a high risk tolerance.*

* **Session Frequency:** Plays frequently with long session durations.
* **Betting Style:** Aggressive; often increases bet size following a win.
* **Bet Range:** €0.50 – €5.00+ (can scale significantly higher).
* **Financials:** * High bankroll: Prepared to lose €500 – €10,000 per session.
    * Frequent "top-ups": May deposit multiple times during a single session.
* **Exit Strategy:** Typically plays until the balance reaches $0.

---

## 3. The Bonus Hunter
*The "Smart Player" focused on system exploitation and ROI.*

* **Session Frequency:** Tied strictly to promotional availability.
* **Betting Style:** Calculated and disciplined; focused on meeting withdrawal requirements.
* **Financials:** * **Strict Rule:** Never deposits without receiving a bonus.
    * **Incentive:** Always initiates play with Free Spin bonuses.
* **Exit Strategy:** Goal-oriented. If the balance reaches a specific threshold ($X), they immediately stop and withdraw.
